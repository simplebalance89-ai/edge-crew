STEP 1 Grok Reasons Freely

Grok gets the full game context -- injuries, matchups, trends, sharp money signals, line movement -- and reasons through it with no JSON constraints. Pure analysis. 2M context window means nothing gets cut.

STEP 2 DeepSeek Formats

DeepSeek-V3.2 takes Grok's raw analysis and structures it into clean JSON: matrix_scores (all 27 variables), edge_question, letter grades. This separation means Grok thinks freely while DeepSeek handles the schema.

STEP 3 Server Recalculates

The server never trusts GPT's math. It takes the 27 matrix scores, applies the real weights, computes: (weighted_sum / max_possible) * 10. Then it runs 8-chain detection for bonus signals. The composite score you see is always server-calculated.

FALLBACK gpt-4.1

If the two-model pipeline fails (Grok timeout, DeepSeek parse error), a single-model fallback using gpt-4.1 catches the game so nothing gets dropped.

Chain Detection Bonuses (8 Chains)

When multiple signals align, the server detects compound patterns and adds bonus points to the composite. Expanded from 5 to 8 chains based on 5-model consensus:

TEMPO TRAP +2.0
tempo_matchup ≥ 8 AND three_pt_reliance ≥ 7 AND public_money_bias ≥ 7
Pace mismatch + public on the fast team + 3PT variance -- book undervalues tempo.
BRAND TAX FADE +2.0
public_money_bias ≥ 8 AND public_brand_fade ≥ 7 AND line_movement ≤ 4
Public >70% on blue blood + line moves WITH public + metrics favor dog. Biggest March market error. Historical +6% ROI.
CINDERELLA SIGNAL +2.0
defensive_efficiency ≥ 7 AND turnover_margin ≥ 7 AND net_ranking_gap ≥ 7 AND recent_form ≥ 7
Mid-major with top-50 D + low TO rate + sustainable shooting -- classic tournament upset profile.
REGRESSION WAVE +2.0
recent_form ≥ 8 AND regression_markers ≥ 7
Hot streak + 2+ regression markers flagged (unsustainable 3PT/FT/TO luck). Fade the streak.
TRAVEL BODY CLOCK +2.0
travel_rest_disparity ≥ 7 AND neutral_site_performance ≥ 6
West Coast team + early EST tip + travel >1000mi. Market undervalues fatigue/time zone. 12% ATS underperformance.
COACHING COMFORT +1.5
coaching_tournament_record ≥ 8 AND tournament_context ≥ 7
Coach 4+ tourney wins vs coach <2 tourney wins. Experience premium unpriced in early rounds.
REF WHISTLE EDGE +1.5
referee_whistle_profile ≥ 7 AND foul_trouble_risk ≥ 7 AND bench_depth ≥ 7
High-foul ref crew + physical underdog + star-dependent favorite. Whistle variance favors depth teams.
TIRED LEGS TAX +1.5
conference_tourney_fatigue ≥ 7 AND travel_rest_disparity ≥ 6 AND bench_depth ≤ 5
3+ conf tourney games + <2 days rest before NCAA Round 1. Fade in 1H spreads -- fatigue shows early.

Alyssa's Math Engine

MATH ENGINE Independent from the AI pipeline. Pure expected value math:

  • Mathurin Screener -- pulls last 5 game logs from ESPN. Computes the FLOOR (lowest of L5) vs the book's line. Floor > line = mispriced.
  • Multi-Book Comparison -- lines from 12+ sportsbooks across us, us2, eu, uk, au regions. Flags gaps > 10 cents ML or > 0.5 spread.
  • Injury Gap Detection -- star OUT, identify which role players absorb minutes/usage. If the book hasn't adjusted their props = free money.
  • EV Calculation -- (our_prob * payout) - (1 - our_prob). Positive EV = bet. Negative = pass.

The Rule

The Mathurin Test: Before placing any bet -- "Can you show a NUMBER that proves the book is wrong?"

YES: "His L5 floor is 21, book has 17.5. Gap is +3.5." → BET IT.
NO: "I think they'll cover." → PASS. That's an opinion, not an edge.

No opinions. Only math. The board IS the data.
